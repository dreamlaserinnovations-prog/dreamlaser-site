Industrial Metal Engraving That Delivers Durability and Compliance

In high-demand industrial environments, the need for precise, durable, and legible equipment identification is critical. Whether you’re a contractor outfitting a new facility, a plant manager overseeing compliance, or an electrician labeling switchgear, custom laser-engraved metal tags offer long-term performance in harsh conditions.

At Dream Laser Innovations in Franklin, Indiana, we specialize in industrial engraving solutions that meet the rigorous standards of manufacturing, construction, and life safety systems. From stainless steel tags to anodized aluminum plates, our laser engraving services are tailored to ensure clarity, durability, and regulatory compliance across sectors.

Stainless Steel Equipment Tags

Stainless steel is a preferred material for equipment tags in demanding industrial environments. Its corrosion resistance and strength make it ideal for identifying valves, pumps, motors, and control panels. Our fiber laser technology creates deep, legible marks that withstand extreme temperatures, chemicals, and abrasion—perfect for food processing plants, chemical facilities, and outdoor installations.

Laser-Etched Electrical Panel Labels

Proper labeling of electrical panels is not only a best practice—it’s an OSHA requirement. We produce custom laser-etched panel labels that are easy to read and permanently marked, ensuring safety and simplifying maintenance. Whether you need engraved stainless steel or UV-stable anodized aluminum, we can match your specifications and mounting requirements.

Hydraulic Calculation Plates for Fire Protection Systems

Fire protection contractors rely on accurate and legible hydraulic calculation signage to meet NFPA code. Our laser-engraved hydraulic calculation plates are produced to order with precise flow, pressure, and system design parameters. Available in stainless steel or anodized aluminum, each plate is built for long-term legibility in exposed environments such as pump rooms, risers, and mechanical spaces.

Anodized Aluminum Serial Plates and Asset Tags

When you need lightweight yet durable identification solutions, anodized aluminum is an excellent choice. The anodizing process seals the engraved surface beneath a hard oxide layer, making it resistant to chemicals, UV, and wear. We produce industrial-grade serial plates containing barcodes, QR codes, serial numbers, and model data—ideal for OEMs and equipment manufacturers tracking assets over time.

Industrial Asset Labeling for Long-Term Traceability

Plant managers and facility engineers depend on clear asset labeling for inventory control, maintenance records, and equipment traceability. We offer custom asset tags in a range of sizes and formats to suit your workflow—whether you need high-contrast black-on-silver data plates or color-coded tags for quick identification. Our laser-engraved solutions ensure your data stays readable for the life of the asset.
