Popular Industrial Engraving Applications

Our metal engraving solutions are trusted by contractors, electricians, fire protection companies, manufacturers, and plant managers across Indiana and the Midwest. Below are the most requested applications we support:

Stainless Steel Equipment Tags

  • Ideal for high-temperature or corrosive environments
  • Durable, rust-resistant, and easy to clean
  • Perfect for machinery ID, valve tags, and safety information

Stainless steel tags are a popular choice in manufacturing plants and fire protection systems due to their high resistance to chemicals and extreme temperatures. Our laser engraving process ensures clear, permanent markings that won’t fade over time.

Electrical Panel Labels

  • Custom sizes and designs for switchboards and control panels
  • High-contrast laser marking for easy readability
  • Compliant with industry labeling standards

Electrical contractors and plant managers rely on us for precise panel labels that improve safety and streamline maintenance. Our engraved metal labels help reduce downtime by quickly identifying circuits and components.

Hydraulic Calculation Plates

  • Essential for fire protection system compliance
  • Laser-etched with precise calculation data
  • Weather-resistant materials for indoor and outdoor use

We work closely with fire protection companies to produce NFPA-compliant hydraulic calculation plates that meet inspection requirements. Each plate is custom engraved with flow and pressure details based on system designs.

Anodized Aluminum Serial Plates

  • Lightweight yet durable material
  • Contrast-rich engraving for serial numbers and barcodes
  • Used in equipment manufacturing and asset tracking

Our anodized aluminum plates are popular among OEMs and manufacturers for labeling equipment with serial numbers, QR codes, and branding. The anodized surface provides excellent durability while maintaining a professional look.

Industrial Asset Labeling

  • Custom solutions for tools, machinery, and facility assets
  • Permanent identification to reduce loss and increase traceability
  • Engraved logos, asset numbers, and safety instructions

From heavy-duty machinery to small tools, asset labeling ensures quick identification and efficient inventory management. We partner with plant managers and facilities teams to develop long-term labeling strategies using engraved metal tags tailored to each asset type.
